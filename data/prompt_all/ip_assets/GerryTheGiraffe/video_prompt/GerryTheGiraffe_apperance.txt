A 360-degree turntable video for “Gerry the Giraffe” focusing on canonical appearance and gentle expressive facial acting.

Character design must match exactly:
- Bright yellow-orange giraffe with irregular dark brown spots.
- Large expressive white eyes with black pupils; small upturned red smile; red nostrils.
- Two dark brown tufted ossicones; large pointed ears (yellow with orange interior).
- Long straight neck; plump rounded body; four long slender legs; dark brown hooves.
- Short tail with dark tuft; subtle dark spiky mane down the neck visible from back.

Shot / motion requirements:
- Full-body, centered, fixed camera; bright cheerful whimsical lighting.
- Slow smooth 360° rotation with clear holds at front/side/back to show spots, ossicones, mane, tail tuft, and hooves.
- Keep shape smooth and rounded; no sharp angles added.

Facial acting requirements:
- Gentle, friendly expression baseline.
- Add subtle but rich eye acting: slow blinks, mild eye widening for curiosity, small gaze shifts, soft smile holds.
