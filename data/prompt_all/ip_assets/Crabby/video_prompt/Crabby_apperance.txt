A 360-degree turntable showcase video for “Crabby” focusing on canonical appearance and eye-based facial acting.

Character design must match exactly:
- Bright stylized anthropomorphic red crab with smooth glossy carapace.
- Oversized spherical white eyes with black pupils and thin red rim.
- Beige underbelly with two small protruding white teeth.
- Eight articulated legs and two large prominent red claws.
- Bright cartoonish 3D style.

Shot / motion requirements:
- Full-body, centered, fixed camera; bright sunny beach-style lighting; clean background.
- Slow smooth 360° rotation with clear pauses at front/side/back to show claws, legs, teeth, and carapace shape.
- No design drift; keep gloss/material consistent.

Facial acting requirements:
- Eyes are the main expression: wide curious stare, slight pupil shifts, blink, mild squint of focus, delighted widening.
- Keep the expression consistent with “curious and alert”.
